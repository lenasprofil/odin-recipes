Sushi Bake

Home Recommendation About Me

Dive into the ultimate comfort food

Sushi Bake is a trendy Japanese-inspired dish that consists of sushi rice, imitation crab meat, and various toppings, baked in a casserole dish until golden and bubbly. It's often served as a fun and easy way to enjoy the flavors of sushi in a more casual setting.

Ingredients

  • 250 g salmon
  • seasoning as preferred
  • 250 g surimi sticks
  • 85 g cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup of (japanese) mayo
  • 1 TBSP of sriracha
  • 500 g cooked rice
  • 2 TBSP of rice vinegar
  • Seaweed

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 200Â°C.
  2. Season the salmon fillet with pepper, salt, garlic powder and paprika powder. Fry it in a pan over medium heat for about 10 minutes, or until it's cooked through.
  3. While the salmon is cooking, shred the surimi sticks into small pieces. Once the salmon is done, shred it into small pieces as well, and mix it with the surimi.
  4. Add the cream cheese, mayonnaise, and sriracha sauce to the salmon and surimi mixture. Mix well until everything is combined evenly.
  5. Add the rice vinegar to the cooked sushi rice and stir well. Spread the rice in an oven-safe dish, creating a flat surface with a spoon.
  6. Sprinkle shredded nori seaweed over the top of the rice, using two sheets in total.
  7. Add the salmon and surimi mixture to the dish, spreading it out evenly over the rice.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbly.
  9. Serve as desired, such as wrapped in seaweed or with sliced avocado or cucumber.
    Enjoy your delicious sushi bake!
