Bobotie

One-Pot Wonder: Easy Bobotie Recipe for Busy Weeknights

Bobotie is a popular South African dish made with spiced minced meat, egg custard topping, and served with rice or bread. It's a unique blend of flavors and textures that's sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg minced beef or lamb
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of minced garlic
  • 2 TBSP curry powder
  • 2 TBSP apricot jam
  • 1 TBSP of chutney
  • 1 TBSP of vinegar
  • 1 TBSP of oil
  • 2 slices of bread soaked in milk
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 beaten eggs
  • 3 eggs
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 180Â°C.
  2. In a large pan, heat the oil over medium heat. Add the onions and garlic and cook until softened.
  3. Add the minced beef or lamb and cook until browned.
  4. Add the curry powder, apricot jam, chutney, vinegar, salt, and pepper. Mix well and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Add the soaked bread and mix well. Transfer the mixture to a baking dish.
  6. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs and milk for the topping. Pour over the meat mixture.
  7. Bake for 40-45 minutes, or until the topping is set and golden brown.
    Serve hot with rice and a side salad and enjoy.
