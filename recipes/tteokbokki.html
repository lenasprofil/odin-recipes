Tteokbokki

Chewy and Spicy: The Perfect Combination of Tteokbokki.

Tteokbokki is a popular Korean street food made with chewy rice cakes, spicy sauce, and various toppings. It's a delicious and filling dish that's perfect for a quick meal or snack.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups anchovy stock dashi, unsalted chicken stock, or water
  • 450 g Korean rice cakes
  • 2-3 sheets Korean fish cakes defrosted and cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 TBSP of soy sauce
  • 1 or 2 TBSP of sugar
  • 1 TBSPkorean chili powder
  • 2 cloves of minced garlic
  • 2 TBSP honey rice syrup, oligo syrup, or your choice of liquid sweetener
  • 3 TBSP of Gochujang (Korean red chili paste)

Steps

  1. Combine the soy sauce, sugar, chili powder, garlic, liquid sweetener and Gochujang in a small bowl.
    If your rice cakes are frozen, defrost them in warm water for 10 minutes prior to cooking.
  2. Add stock to a large pan over medium heat and bring to a boil. Add rice cakes and sauce and stir to combine. Cook for about 10 minutes until the sauce is reduced, add fish cakes, and cook for another 2 minutes.
    Garnish and enjoy!
